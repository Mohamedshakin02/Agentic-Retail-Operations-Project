# KPI Definitions: Agentic Retail Operations Copilot

## Overview

This document defines all Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used in the retail analytics system. These KPIs are used for:

- Sales performance analysis
- Inventory monitoring
- Risk detection
- Forecast evaluation
- Business decision-making

All KPIs are calculated using Python logic in the `src/` modules, not by the LLM.

---

## Sales KPIs

### 1. Revenue

**Definition:**
Total monetary value of sales.

**Formula:**
revenue = units_sold × price

**Code Reference:**
Implemented in `feature_engineering.py`

**Business Meaning:**
Indicates how much income is being generated by a product or store.

---

### 2. Average Selling Price (ASP)

**Definition:**
Average price at which products are sold.

**Formula:**
average_selling_price = revenue / units_sold

**Business Meaning:**
Helps evaluate pricing strategy and discount effectiveness.

---

## Inventory KPIs

### 3. Inventory Level

**Definition:**
Current stock available for a product.

**Column Source:**
`inventory_level`

**Business Meaning:**
Indicates how much stock is available for sale.

---

### 4. Inventory Cover Days

**Definition:**
Number of days current inventory can last based on recent sales.

**Formula:**
inventory_cover_days = inventory_level / average_daily_sales

**Code Reference:**
```python
cover_days = current_inventory / average_daily_sales
